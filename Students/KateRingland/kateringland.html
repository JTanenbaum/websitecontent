Kathryn E. Ringland

About

kateringland.com

Kathryn (Kate) is currently a Ph.D. Candidate in Informatics at University of California, Irvine. Her areas of interest include: Human Computer Interaction, Computer Supported Collaborative Work, Ubiquitous Computing, Assistive Technology, and Technology for Individuals with Autism. She is interested in looking at the collaborative play, embodiment, and ways to design accessible technology.
