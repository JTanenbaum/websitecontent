Amanda Cullen

Amanda Cullen comes to UCI from the Panhandle of Florida. Her previous degrees include a bachelor's and a master's in Cultural Anthropology from the University fo West Florida and a master's in Public Service from the University of Arkansas- Clinton School of Public Service. Her personal website can be found at http://sites.uci.edu/amandallcullen/

Research

Amanda's research interests include: video games, digital cultures, digital media, social media, fandom, sexuality, and gender. Amanda conducts her work under the tutelage of Dr. Bonnie Ruberg and Dr. Aaron Trammell.
